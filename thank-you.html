According to the Canadian Bureau for International Education (2016), one of the top 3 reasons why international students choose Canada is because of its reputation as a tolerant and non-discriminatory society.

From the creators of They Are Your Classmates, Veronica and Charlene:

Thank you for opening your mind and heart to your fellow classmates and taking the time to experience their stories and perspectives. We hope there is something—be it sympathy, thought, perspective, or anything else—that you can take away from this experience. If you were impacted by your classmates' stories, we hope that you might pass them on to others.
