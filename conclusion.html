According to the Canadian Bureau for International Education (2016), one of the top 3 reasons why international students choose Canada is because of its reputation as a tolerant and non-discriminatory society.

Though Vivian, Andy, and Ruiwen speak about their struggles in Canadian schools, they have all expressed to us that they are grateful to be living and studying in a safe, caring country like Canada. They understand that there are negative assumptions about them, but do not fault people for those assumptions because it results from the disconnect between Canadian students and Chinese international students, which is the responsibility of both groups to bridge.
